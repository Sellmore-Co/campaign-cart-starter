Why You Need A Green Powder

Better Than A Multivitamin

The nutrients within multivitamins are artificial. As a result, your digestive system will only absorb a fraction of its nutrients because it doesn't recognize it the same way it would with food or a high-quality green powder (derived from food). Typically, green powders are more easily absorbed than multivitamins.

Contain Nutrient-Dense Superfoods

Superfoods are high in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to help support immune function, promote energy and weight management, and reduce the signs of aging.

Exposure To More Food Diversity & Micronutrients

Previous generations ate a wider variety of foods than we do — approximately 20 different species per day, leading to more micronutrients and phytonutrients. If you're not eating 20 different foods per day, a nutritious green powder can help you get there.

Combat Declining Nutritional Value

Over the last several decades, modern farming methods (pesticides, fertilizers, etc.) have depleted the nutrients from the topsoil, producing less nutritious fruits and vegetables than in our grandparent's time, but green powders can help fill these gaps.
How To Use A Green Powder

Green powders are the best way to cover all your nutritional bases in one simple scoop. Many people enjoy mixing it with their morning glass of water, tea, or smoothie.

The combination of superfoods and gut-boosting probiotics can elevate your health and meet your food diversity goal. This simple morning ritual helps customers stay consistent with their green powder intake, producing the best results.

What to Avoid

Cereal Grasses

Many top brands use cereal grasses to add more quantity to their servings, however, cereal grasses such as wheatgrass, barley, oat, or rye cannot be properly digested by our stomachs. These grasses contain lectins that can cause occasional digestive issues, particularly for people who are gluten-sensitive.

Fruit Powder

Fruit is usually high in sugar. Consuming too much of it in a green powder without fiber can cause your blood sugar levels to spike. Not to mention coming off a sugar high can leave you feeling drained.
What Else To Consider

More Ingredients Isn't Always Better

The more ingredients in a formula the higher the chance those ingredients could counteract another ingredient. In which case, you'll be paying for extra ingredients that don't have much impact. When it comes to ingredients it's usually better to have quality over quantity.

Real Results Take Time

It's important to know that dramatic changes to your health won't happen overnight. It can take months for significant effects to take place. Month three is when many people report seeing a “night and day” difference in their energy levels, immunity, and digestion. So if you want the full effect of a green powder then stick with it for at least three months to feel real results.

Natural Sweeteners

One of the best parts about high-quality green powders: they taste great! Find one with rich, robust natural flavors from xylitol, monk fruit, or stevia. These sweeteners taste good and have zero sugar.

Full Satisfaction Guarantee

On top of letting you shop around for the perfect detox blend, a full satisfaction guarantee demonstrates a company’s faith in its product. The longer the guarantee, the better. Look for satisfaction guarantees for at least 90 days.
